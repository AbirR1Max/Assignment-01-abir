Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation sparks progress, transforming ideas into reality and challenges into opportunities. It's the heartbeat of growth and the key to shaping a better future.

Sustainability

Sustainability is the practice of meeting current needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet theirs. It emphasizes preserving natural resources, reducing waste, and promoting eco-friendly solutions.

Infrastructure

Investing in sustainable infrastructure ensures long-term resilience, driving innovation and improving quality of life for communities.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the key to unlocking your true potential and embracing your unique strengths. When you believe in yourself, you ignite a spark that turns challenges into opportunities.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

Date: 05/01/2025 Time: 10.25 PM

Bangabandhu Satellite-1 is Bangladesh's first geostationary communication satellite, launched on May 11, 2018. Named after the Father of the Nation, Sheikh Mujibur Rahman, it symbolizes the country's progress in space technology. The satellite enhances telecommunications, broadcasting, and internet services, especially in remote and underserved areas. Operated by Bangladesh Communication Satellite Company Limited (BCSCL), it contributes to national development by boosting digital connectivity, disaster management, and socio-economic growth, marking a significant milestone in Bangladesh's technological advancement.
